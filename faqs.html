Common Questions

Properties

What types of properties do you offer?
We offer a wide range of properties including apartments, condos, townhouses, and single-family homes. Our portfolio includes both rental and purchase options to suit different needs and budgets.
How do I schedule a property viewing?
You can schedule a property viewing through our website, by calling our office, or by contacting one of our agents directly. We offer both in-person and virtual viewing options.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve the metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. For specific neighborhoods, please check our website or contact our office for the most up-to-date information.

Legal & Contracts

What documents do I need to rent a property?
Typically, you'll need a valid ID, proof of income, and references. Some properties may require additional documentation. Our agents will provide you with a complete checklist during the application process.
How long is the standard lease agreement?
Our standard lease agreements are typically 12 months, but we also offer flexible terms ranging from 6 to 24 months depending on the property and landlord preferences.
