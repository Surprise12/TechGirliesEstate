Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate real estate professionals and technology experts, Tech Girlies Estate emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between traditional real estate practices and modern technological innovation.

What started as a small boutique agency in Pretoria has grown into a leading real estate force across Gauteng, North West, and Free State. Our unique approach combines deep market knowledge with cutting-edge technology, ensuring our clients receive the best of both worlds.

Today, we're proud to have helped hundreds of families and investors find their perfect properties while maintaining our core values of transparency, innovation, and client-first service.
